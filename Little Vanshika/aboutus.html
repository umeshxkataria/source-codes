About Us

Elevating childhood with love, innovation, and care—because your little ones deserve the best.

Who We Are

Little Vanshika is an initiative by Nayosha Care Foundation, dedicated to redefining baby and child care. We partner with the world’s most trusted brands to provide high-quality childcare, beauty, and wellness products across India.

Our passion is to empower parents with safe, innovative, and premium products that nurture a child’s happiness and well-being.

Our Trusted Brands

We partner with globally recognized brands like Cetaphil, Mamaearth, Himalaya, Agaro, Renee Cosmetics, NIVEA, and Forest Essentials to bring the highest quality products for your child’s well-being.

Every product is curated with love, expertise, and a deep understanding of your baby’s needs.

What We Offer

Gentle baby skincare and bathing essentials.

Nutritious baby food and feeding solutions.

Organic, Ayurvedic, and herbal baby products.

Parenting guidance and wellness support.

Part of a Bigger Vision

We are proud to be a part of the Nayosha Care Foundation, working alongside brands like Navyosha Entertainments, Redsi International Fashion, Navyosha Spice Farm, and Redsi Aqua International. Together, we create a positive impact across industries.
